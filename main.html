My Process

Upload

View instructions Once your music is recorded the way you want it, bounce all your tracks out separately, zip them in a folder, and share them via Google Drive or Dropbox using kurtis.a.roy@gmail.com. It's also helpful for me if you include a few inspiration songs to give me a clearer direction of your goals.

If you're using Logic Pro:
You can send the session files to me in a zip folder. Make sure to delete all unused project files to reduce the size.

If sending from another DAW:
It's really helpful for me if all your stems start and stop at the same time. For example, if you have a guitar solo happening at 2:30, bounce the track so there's an empty waveform block starting at the beginning and ending at 2:30. This is a fool-proof way to ensure everything lines up correctly and saves a lot of time.

Mix

View instructions I will organize and clean up your stems, perform any quantization on the drums and other instruments (if needed) and tune your vocals if requested. Then, I will transform your track from a rough mix to a polished first pass. Once the first pass is complete, it is run through a variety of speakers to ensure it will translate well no matter the output. Then, it is sent to you for feedback!

Revise

View instructions Music is subjective, and as much as we both would like to nail the first pass, this typically never happens - and that's ok! I am not a "what I say goes" engineer - I want to hear ALL your opinions! I offer unlimited mix revisions in order to ensure that we both are satisfied.

With that in mind, please understand that there are certain elements I can't control - such as how the track was produced and recorded. No amount of mixing can fix recording errors.

Download

View instructions After discussing and completing mix revisions, I will send you a high quality .WAV file containing your finished mix. Your mix will be at the appropriate levels and contain enough headroom to ensure that a mastering engineer can do their best work. The rest is up to you!

About Kurt

Hi! I'm a freelance audio engineer and indie pop musician living in Seattle, Washington. I have more than ten years' experience recording, mixing, and mastering, and my work has seen over three million streams on Spotify and Apple Music.

I would love the opportunity to mix your single, EP, or full album. In my home studio, I use high quality converters, studio monitors, and outboard gear to ensure top-tier professionalism. Your satisfication as an artist is my top priority!

To get started, send me an email with a brief description of your goals and I'll get back to you right away. Looking forward to working with you!
